Description of me:

Trusting in my heart, since it feeds me lies,
Conquering the depths but in dreams of skies,
Spoiled from head to toe, living white and clear,
Seeing every shadow as my deepest fear,
Locking feelings up inside, ready to explode,
Born for fine translations, not for lines of code!
Having all the options, seeing it as hell,
Living in the moment doesn't ring a bell,
Craving for some daylight with the Moon in hand,
Since my aspirations break but never bend!
No single zero in my life of ones,
In the race for perfect every joy is gone,
And my soul is starving, since my brain is full
With unneeded knowledge left from freaking school.
I am good for nothing, but still filled with pride
Like a crappy function needing override,
Trading light for darkness, that's for what I strive...
...Even if the world ends, I'll be still alive.
